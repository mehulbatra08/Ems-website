About Us

Founded in 1999, our company has been a trusted name in Electronic manufacturing services (EMS) for over 26 years. Based in Singapore we specialize in design, assembly, production, and testing of high-quality electronic components and PCB assemblies.

With a commitment to innovation and precision, we have grown into a leading EMS provider, serving industries such as automotive, telecommunications, medical devices, and consumer electronics. Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert team ensure that every product meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.

At the heart of our success is a dedication to customer satisfaction. We work closely with our clients to develop customized solutions, leveraging the latest technologies to optimize efficiency and performance.

As we continue to evolve, we remain focused on sustainability, cutting-edge advancements, and delivering excellence in electronic manufacturing. Whether you need prototyping, mass production, or specialized engineering support, we are here to bring your vision to life.
